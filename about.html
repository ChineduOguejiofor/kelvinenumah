BIO

Kelvin Enumah is an Engineer, Poet and a Neuroeducationist. He is an Incorporated Engineer (IEng) of the Engineering Council UK (ECUK) and a member of the Energy Institute (EI). Kelvin holds an Affiliateship award in Engineering from the City and Guilds of London Institute, he is also a certified City and Guilds competency/learning assessor. He is a graduate of the Lagos Business School Non-Profit Leadership and Management Certificate Programme with a lot of interest in the social sector. Kelvin bridges sides due to his acquired family of competencies.
